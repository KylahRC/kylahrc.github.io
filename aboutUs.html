About Braids by Nicole

In the eighth grade, I observed a trend among female students where they wore Dutch braids, which I found aesthetically pleasing and desired to replicate.

However, I encountered challenges in achieving the desired outcome, prompting me to seek alternative methods.

Through experimentation and research, I discovered that the technique was not as intricate as initially perceived.





As my expertise in braiding developed, I have conducted extensive research by viewing various instructional videos on YouTube.

Furthermore, I gained practical experience by practicing on friends and family members.

My passion for braiding led me to establish my own business, where I now offer braiding services as both a hobby and a profession.
